Story

As a Mechanical Engineer turned Full Stack Software Engineer, I enjoy collaborating with other engineers and developers and tackling challenging problems together. What I like most is solving complex problems all over the stack.

I specialize in JavaScript, ReactJS, ReduxJS, Ruby on Rails, as well as in other technologies like GraphQL, Docker, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Express.js, and Node.js.

After graduating from UCLA with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering, I started my career with ORANO USA. I worked 2 years on automation hardware before transitioning to a Software Engineer role, where I developed control systems software for 5+ years.

I recently attended App Academy's 24-weeks course, where I spent rigorous 1,600+ hours sharpening my web development skills and built 4 portfolio projects. Those include a fully responsive e-commerce app that I built over 2.5 weeks, as well as a simple real-time messaging app leveraging GraphQL/Apollo mutations and subscriptions developed in less than a week.

Outside of Web Development, I am also passionate about emerging tech (e.g blockchain, AI) and their impact on international economics + social policies. As I lived in France for 17 years, I like exploring what makes each culture unique and exciting!
