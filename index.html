ABOUT ME

As a Mechanical Engineer turned Full Stack Software Engineer, I enjoy collaborating with other engineers and developers and tackling challenging problems together. What I love most is working on problems that require perseverance and creative thinking.

After graduating from UCLA with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering, I worked for ORANO USA for 7 years. I spent 2 years working as a Mechanical Engineer, then fully transitioned to a Software Engineer role, where I developed control systems software for 5+ years.

I recently attended App Academy's 24-weeks course, where I spent rigorous 1,600+ hours sharpening my web development skills and built 4 portfolio projects. Those include a fully responsive e-commerce app that I built over 2.5 weeks, as well as a simple real-time messaging app leveraging GraphQL/Apollo mutations and subscriptions developed in less than a week.

I am proficient in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and React.js & Redux.js, as well as in other technologies like GraphQL, Docker, MongoDB, Express.js, and Node.js.
